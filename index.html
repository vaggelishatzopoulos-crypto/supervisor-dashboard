Welcome to the
F&B Performance Dashboard
A structured, transparent platform for evaluating and developing F&B supervisors at Ikos Aria. Every evaluation is grounded in our group values and operational standards — so that feedback is always fair, consistent, and meaningful.
How it works
Three pillars · one composite score · displayed as a percentage
40%
Operational KPIs
Measurable outcomes: LQA/audit, guest satisfaction, cost control, revenue performance, complaint rate. Enter the actual number and the score is calculated automatically.
35%
Supervisor Competencies
How you lead: decision making, organization, communication, creativity, and staff development. Rated 1–5 with full anchor descriptions for every level.
25%
Values Alignment (RACE+)
Who you are: Respectful, Authentic, Creative, Passionate, Responsible, Excellent. These are the Sani/Ikos Group values — not aspirations, but daily commitments.
Performance bands
All scores are converted to a percentage — because percentages need no explanation
Transparency
Evaluations are not done in the dark
After each evaluation, the manager generates a personal scorecard link — unique to each supervisor. It can be opened on any device to see exactly how you were scored: every KPI benchmark, every competency anchor, every values descriptor. Nothing is hidden. The goal is not to judge — it is to build a shared understanding of where you stand and where you can grow.
